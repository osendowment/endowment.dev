## Become a Member

Donate **$1,000 or more** in a year to become an OSE Member. Members can:

- Advise the Board on strategy and the grant-making model.
- Appoint community-nominated board directors.
- Vote on key decisions.
- Participate in closed events.

Each $1,000 grants one year of membership. Full details in the [membership policy](/docs/membership-policy/).

## For organizations

Corporate and institutional donors are welcome. Organizations don't receive governance rights — a deliberate design choice to preserve OSE's long-term neutrality and alignment with the global open source community. [See ways to give](/docs/giving/) or [contact us](mailto:donations@endowment.dev) about stock, private stock, international wires, or DAF grants.
