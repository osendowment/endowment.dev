# Nominate a Board Director Candidate

Know someone who would strengthen the OSE board? Anyone can highlight a worthy candidate, and self-nominations are welcome too!

→ [Submit a nomination](/nominate-board/)

## Who can nominate

Anyone. You need not be a donor or Member to put a name forward, and you may nominate yourself. Tell us who they are and why they fit.

## Membership requirement

Directors must be OSE [Members](/about#governance) — donors giving at least **$1,000/year**. Candidates can join after nomination, so nominate first.

## What the board does

The main governing body of the Open Source Endowment: approves grants, appoints the Executive Director, selects investment strategy, etc. Details in the [bylaws](/docs/bylaws/).

## How election works

We review nominations on a rolling basis against OSE's needs — fundraising, investment management, open source expertise — and interview and invite the most promising candidates to join. Directors are elected for a three-year term.

## What we ask for

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| Your name | The nominator |
| Your email | The nominator |
| Candidate name | Your own name for a self-nomination |
| Candidate profile URL | LinkedIn, Wikipedia, or any public profile |
| Comments | Why they would strengthen the board — track record, experience, links |
