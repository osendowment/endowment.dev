# Ways to Give

OSE is a US 501(c)(3) tax-exempt public charity — donations are [tax-deductible](https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/charitable-organizations/charitable-contribution-deductions) for US donors.

## Donation methods

| Method | Description | Action |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| **ACH or US Bank Wire** | US bank transfers are the most cost-effective way to donate. **ACH transfers are free** — no fees from Every.org or us. Wire transfers also carry no platform fee. | [Donate via every.org](https://www.every.org/osendowment#/donate/bank) |
| **International Bank Wire** | We accept international wire transfers in [almost all currencies](https://support.mercury.com/hc/en-us/articles/34750478414740-Receiving-non-USD-payments) to a dedicated account at Mercury bank. Non-USD amounts are converted to USD automatically. Fees depend on your bank; **no fee for receiving USD payments** on our side. | [Contact us](mailto:donations@endowment.dev?subject=International%20Bank%20Wire) |
| **Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay** | We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Processing fee: **2.2% + $0.30** (Amex: **3.5%**), +1% for non-US cards. | [Donate via every.org](https://www.every.org/osendowment#/donate/card) |
| **Cryptocurrency** | Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major cryptocurrencies. Every.org automatically converts crypto to USD upon receipt. Platform fee: **1%**. | [Donate via every.org](https://www.every.org/osendowment#/donate/crypto) |
| **PayPal** | Donate using your PayPal or Venmo balance or linked payment methods. Processing fee: **1.99%** (+1.5% for non-US payments). | [Donate via every.org](https://www.every.org/osendowment#/donate/paypal) |
| **Donor-Advised Fund (DAF)** | Grant from your DAF directly to the Open Source Endowment Foundation (EIN 33-3502715). Every.org integrates with major DAF providers including Fidelity Charitable, Schwab Charitable, and BNY Mellon. **No fees.** | [Donate via every.org](https://www.every.org/osendowment#/donate/daf) |
| **Public Stock** | Donate publicly traded shares directly. Every.org handles the transfer and automatically converts stock to USD. **No platform fee.** | [Donate via every.org](https://www.every.org/osendowment#/donate/stocks) |
| **Private Stock** | We accept private stock, including unallocated employee stock options — a convenient option for early-stage tech startups and founders who have not yet exited. | [Contact us](mailto:donations@endowment.dev?subject=Private%20Stock%20Donation) |
| **Anything else?** | We aim to accept donations in all ways convenient for our donors. API and payroll integrations (like Gusto Giving) are on our roadmap. Don't see your preferred method? Create an issue in our GitHub repo, and we'll look into adding it. | [Create issue on GitHub](https://github.com/osendowment/foundation/issues/new) |

## Tax-deductibility notes

OSE's EIN is **33-3502715**. For US donors, contributions are tax-deductible to the full extent permitted by law. Individuals can deduct up to 60% of adjusted gross income for cash donations; corporations up to 25% of taxable income. Unused deductions carry forward up to five years.
